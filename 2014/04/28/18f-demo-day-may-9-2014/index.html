18F Demo Day

April 28, 2014

Tagged / demo day / culture /

by 18F

Who: GSA’s 18F

What: 18F Demo Day

When: Friday, May 9, 2014 at 9:00 a.m. (Registration opens at 8:15 a.m.)

Where: GSA Headquarters; 1800 F Street, NW, Washington, D.C. [map]

How: Open to the government and the public. Space is limited, so please register now.

Delivering on GSA's mission to expand opportunities for small business, we're hosting 18F Demo Day Friday, May 9, 2014 at 9:00 a.m. Attendees from across the federal government and the small business and tech communities will get a behind-the-scenes look at our projects that are currently underway.

Hatched at GSA HQ in Washington, D.C., 18F is building tools for a 21st century digital government. Come see what the team is working on and meet the folks delivering these exciting new services.

By connecting the people, principles, and methodologies of the technology startup world with the government’s leaders in innovation, 18F seeks to improve the way agencies tackle their missions. Our team is designed to provide the American people the digital services they expect from a 21st century government.

Space is limited, so register today to join us at GSA on May 9.. We can't wait to see you there!
